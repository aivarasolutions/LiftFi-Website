# Lift Fi - Official Brand Identity Package

This document accompanies the final, polished brand identity for **Lift Financial Holdings LLC**, based on the approved Crest + Holding Architecture direction. The identity centers on a metallic gold shield containing a gold canopy beam and three white pillars—symbolizing wealth protection (the crest) and a parent company organizing multiple business units (the architecture)—paired with a premium modern serif wordmark and a champagne gold subtitle.

## Typography

The wordmark "Lift Fi" uses a premium modern serif in the spirit of Playfair Display or Didot—refined but contemporary. The subtitle "LIFT FINANCIAL HOLDINGS LLC" uses a clean, widely tracked geometric sans-serif in the spirit of Montserrat. For brand documents, pair the serif for headlines with the sans-serif for body copy and labels.

## Usage Notes

The gold crest icon is designed to work standalone at any size, from a 16-pixel favicon to large-format signage. On dark backgrounds, use the gold-and-white versions; on light backgrounds, use the white-background or one-color black versions. Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the canopy beam, and never apply glow, shadow, bevel, or gradient effects. The SVG file is the scalable master and should be provided to any designer or print vendor producing final materials; note that the SVG text renders with system fonts, so vendors should install or substitute the specified typefaces for pixel-perfect output.
