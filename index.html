Growth Roadmap

A Phased Path to Scale

1

Organize

Confirm all LLCs and DBAs, gather documents, clean up bookkeeping, and create current and target ownership charts.

2

Activate Lift

Open banking, create bookkeeping, build financial dashboard, create company email/domain, and draft internal agreements.

3

Restructure

Review with CPA and attorney, transfer ownership interests where appropriate, update operating agreements, and decide whether IPM should become a standalone LLC.

4

Build for Funding

Create investor materials, pitch deck, financial reports, partner overview, acquisition strategy, and portfolio dashboard.

5

Scale

Acquire assets, launch new ventures, raise capital when appropriate, expand real estate and technology operations, and grow the business ecosystem.
