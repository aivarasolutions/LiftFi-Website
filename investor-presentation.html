Executive Summary

A Private Holding Company Built for the Long Term

Lift Financial Holdings LLC is a diversified private holding company focused on acquiring, building, and operating technology-enabled businesses.

Our mission is to centralize ownership, operations, technology, and capital allocation across complementary industries to create long-term shareholder value.

Acquire

Pursue ownership of durable, cash-generating businesses in sectors we understand deeply.

Build

Develop technology, systems, and brands internally when building creates more value than buying.

Operate

Run each company with discipline, shared infrastructure, and a long-term ownership mindset.
