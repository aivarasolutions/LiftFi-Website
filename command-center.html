Internal Overview

Command Center

A working summary of where the ecosystem stands today, where it is headed, and the immediate steps required to get there.

Structure

Current vs. Target Structure

Current Structure

  • Kevin Jackson directly owns interests in multiple operating companies and brands
  • Lift Financial Holdings LLC formed as the intended parent entity
  • Some brands operate as DBAs under existing LLCs
  • Ownership, documents, and bookkeeping managed per-company

Target Structure

  • Kevin Jackson owns Lift Financial Holdings LLC
  • Lift holds ownership (or Kevin's ownership interest) in all operating companies
  • Each operating company keeps separate banking, bookkeeping, and contracts
  • Lift manages treasury, capital allocation, governance, and investor readiness
